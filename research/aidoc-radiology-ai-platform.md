## 1. Company Profile

### Overview
Aidoc is an Israeli-American technology company specializing in artificial intelligence for medical imaging analysis. The company develops "always-on AI" solutions that assist radiologists by automatically analyzing medical scans to detect and prioritize acute abnormalities.

### Leadership & Founding
- **CEO:** Elad Walach (Co-founder)
- **CTO:** Michael Braginsky (Co-founder)
- **VP:** Guy Reiner (Co-founder)

All three founders are graduates of the elite Israeli Defense Force's Talpiot program and leveraged their AI research expertise to address inefficiencies in medical diagnostics.

### Company Size
- **Employees:** ~488 total
- **Global Reach:** 150+ health systems, 1,600+ hospitals worldwide

## 2. Product Offerings

### aiOS™ Platform
Aidoc's enterprise-grade clinical AI platform that unifies systems, teams, and data to improve healthcare workflows.

**Key Features:**
- Vendor-agnostic architecture
- Real-time AI-powered insights
- Smart filters for urgency-based prioritization
- Triage of acute and non-acute findings
- Single integration scales AI enterprise-wide
- 69% of customers run non-Aidoc models on aiOS

### CARE™ Foundation Model
**Clinical AI Reasoning Engine (CARE)** - The first clinical-grade foundation model in healthcare with FDA-cleared solutions.

**Capabilities:**
- Trained on multimodal medical data
- Enables new indication development up to 20x faster
- Powers FDA-cleared applications
- Two CARE-powered solutions already FDA cleared (Rib Fractures, Aortic Dissection)

### Clinical Solutions Portfolio

| Solution Category | Capabilities |
|-------------------|--------------|
| **Radiology AI** | Image-based triage and quantification |
| **Stroke Care** | LVO detection, time-critical alerts |

| **Neurological** | ICH, brain aneurysm detection |
| **Trauma** | C-spine fractures, rib fractures |
