## Executive Summary

Abridge has emerged as the dominant player in healthcare ambient AI documentation, achieving **Best in KLAS 2025** recognition and scaling to **200+ health system deployments** supporting **50+ million annual clinical conversations**. The company has secured enterprise agreements with virtually every major U.S. academic medical center and health system, demonstrating unprecedented market penetration with **95%+ clinician retention rates** and measurable outcomes across burnout reduction, documentation efficiency, and revenue impact.
