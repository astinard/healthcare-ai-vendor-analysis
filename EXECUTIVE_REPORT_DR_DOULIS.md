## Part 9: Competitive Threats & Market Dynamics

### Epic "Art for Clinicians" (August 2025)

| Factor | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Announcement | Epic UGM August 2025 |
| Technology | Microsoft Dragon AI + Epic Cosmos (300M patient records) |
| Distribution | 42% of US hospitals use Epic |
| Pricing | Expected included or low-cost add-on |
| Impact | Potential commoditization of basic ambient AI |

**Implications:**
- Third-party vendors differentiating with beyond-the-note capabilities
- Abridge emphasizing proprietary AI stack vs Epic/Microsoft
- Non-Epic EHR customers become more valuable

### Market Consolidation Indicators

- Microsoft acquired Nuance ($19.7B, 2022)
- Abridge valuation trajectory suggests potential acquisition target or IPO
- Smaller vendors may consolidate or exit
- EHR vendors adding native capabilities

## Part 10: Capability Gaps by Vendor

### Nuance/Microsoft
- Innovation pace slower than startups
- KLAS satisfaction below Abridge
- Premium pricing vs new entrants

### Abridge
- US/Epic-centric - limited international
- Australian presence: None
- EU data residency: Not confirmed
- Non-Epic EHRs: Limited

### Nabla
- Smaller scale than Nuance/Abridge
- Limited KLAS coverage (newer to US)
- Australian regulatory status unclear
- Enterprise reference base smaller in US

### Heidi Health
- Hospital/surgical workflows less proven (GP-focused)
- Beyond-the-note capabilities limited
- International coding (non-Australian) unclear
- Scale for large enterprise unproven

### Commure/Augmedix
- US-focused - limited international presence
- HCA-centric deployment history
- Hybrid human+AI model transitioning
- Public information limited (private company)

### Ambience
- International presence limited
- Language support narrower
- Public information more limited

## Part 11: Key Questions for Vendor Evaluation

### For All Vendors
1. International coding support (AR-DRG, HRG, T2A)?
2. Data residency options by region?
3. Implementation timeline for multi-country deployment?
4. Language quality validation methodology?
5. Regulatory pathway by jurisdiction?

### For Nabla
1. Australian TGA pathway and timeline?
2. Nordic language clinical validation data?
3. Enterprise reference customers at Ramsay's scale?
4. Local support model for APAC?

### For Heidi Health
1. Hospital/inpatient workflows (beyond GP)?
2. Surgical specialty support depth?
3. International coding (non-Australian)?
4. Enterprise scalability at 100+ hospitals?

### For Abridge
1. International expansion plans and timeline?
2. Non-Epic EHR integration roadmap?
3. EU/Australia regulatory pathway?
4. Data residency outside US?
